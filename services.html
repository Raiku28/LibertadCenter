We are the primary health center of the Libertad District, operated by the Butuan City Health Office. We deliver quality health services for free.

  • BUTUAN CITY—The constituents of Libertad in Butuan City, Agusan del Norte, led by its local government leaders and Department of Health (DOH) officials, came together to celebrate the inauguration of the town’s newly rehabilitated rural health unit (RHU).
  • The community welcomed the unveiling of Libertad Main Health Center, which now has new and improved offices, consultation rooms, treatment rooms, dental clinic, laboratory, labor room, breastfeeding area and birthing clinic.
  • This project was made possible by BDO Foundation, the corporate social responsibility arm of BDO Unibank. Libertad Main Health Center is one of several health centers rehabilitated by the foundation in Mindanao.
